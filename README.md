### **Visual 1: Recency Performance by Customer Segment**

**Data:**
- **At Risk:** R_score = 1 (haven't purchased recently)
- **Loyal Customers:** R_score = 2, 3, 4 (active)
- **Others:** R_score = 2, 3 (moderate activity)

**Key Insights:**
üö® **CRITICAL:** At Risk customers have R_score = 1  
‚úÖ Loyal Customers maintain R_scores of 2-4  
‚ö†Ô∏è "Others" need nurturing to become Loyal  

**Recency Score Meaning:**
- **R=1:** Purchased 76-100 days ago (DANGER ZONE)
- **R=2:** Purchased 51-75 days ago
- **R=3:** Purchased 26-50 days ago
- **R=4:** Purchased 0-25 days ago (EXCELLENT)

**Business Action:**
- **At Risk (R=1):**
  - Immediate win-back email campaign
  - Offer: 20% off + free shipping
  - Urgency: "We miss you! 7-day exclusive offer"
  - SMS follow-up after 3 days if no response
  
- **Loyal (R=2-4):**
  - Monthly newsletter with early access
  - Loyalty points: 1 point = $1 spent
  - Exclusive sales 24-48 hours before public
  - VIP customer service line

- **Others (R=2-3):**
  - Move to Loyal tier with engagement campaign
  - Personalized product recommendations
  - "Refer a friend" program with incentives
